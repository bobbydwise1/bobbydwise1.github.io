About Myself

Summary

My entire career has been about solving complex technical and engineering problems.

My background is in electronics engineering and electrical repair. I am now pursuing the next logical step - computer programming.

The skills of electrical repair and electrical engineering are a natural cross over into programming. There's creating a product / solution for your customers and meeting their requirements, while ensuring that those products are economical and profitable solutions. There's reading a document that abstractly represents a solution to a real world problem, testing the product, fixing bugs in the product, and supporting the product and your customers.

Authoring electrical schematics is also a natural crossover into authoring code. The electrical schematic "code" is made of complex symbols instead, but the methods of creating, testing, and troubleshooting the "code" is similar.

In addition to already been in the field of electrical engineering, I'm also a U.S. Army Veteran. I have personal qualities such as discpline under pressure, a hard work ethos, taking responsibility and accountability, and paying attention to details.

I can also solve a Rubiks cube.... but only in 5 minutes.
