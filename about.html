About Myself

My entire career has been about solving complex technical and engineering problems. I started my path in electronics repair, then went into electrical engineering. I am now pursuing the next logical step - computer programming. In my view, this is the extension of my already existing engineering skills into another domain.

The skills of electrical repair and electrical engineering are a natural cross over into programming. There's creating a product / solution for your customers and meeting their requirements, while ensuring that those products are economical and profitable solutions. There's reading a document that abstractly represents a solution to a real world problem, testing the product, fixing bugs in the product, and supporting the product and your customers.

I am also a U.S. Army Veteran, and I have personal qualities such as discpline under pressure, a hard work ethos, taking responsibility and accountability, and paying attention to details.
