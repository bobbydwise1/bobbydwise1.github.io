Summary

The game of Pig Dice, created early in my programming coursework.

In Pig Dice, you must roll a set of die. As long as you don't exceed a certian dice roll, the current dice roll will be added to your temporary score.

You can keep re-rolling the dice, as long as you don't exceed the threshold value. However, if you do exceed the value, your turn is over immediately, and you don't get to keep any of the temporary points.

If you call "hold", the current temporary score is added to your final score, and your turn ends.
